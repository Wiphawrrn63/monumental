Crowdsource cultural heritage data around the world

Welcome to Monumental GLAM – the tool to explore cultural heritage institutions around the world.

Enter the name of an institution in the search box above (for example, Rijksmuseum) to learn more, or explore institutions by entering a geographic region (for example, Berlin). You can also click an example item or city below.

The tool displays information and multimedia about cultural heritage institutions gathered through Wikidata, Wikipedia and Wikimedia Commons. It is a modified version of Monumental and was developed as a part of the FindingGLAMs project by Wikimedia Sverige, with the goal of encouraging new users to contribute to the crowdsourcing campaign.
